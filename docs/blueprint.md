# **App Name**: Futebol Lucrativo PRO

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Sales Content Display: Render all sales copy, including headline, subheadline, problem, solution, benefits, examples, and offer, with aggressive and persuasive styling to maximize engagement and drive conversion.
- Video Sales Letter (VSL) Player: Embed a large, prominent video player for the 5-minute sales video, configured for optimal viewing on various devices to convey the product's value aggressively.
- Conversion-Optimized CTA System: Implement multiple, large, visually striking call-to-action buttons across the page, each uniformly labeled 'QUERO ACESSAR O FORNECEDOR AGORA', linking directly to the checkout page, with a sticky version for mobile users.
- Urgency & Scarcity Mechanism: Display a prominent urgency countdown timer and scarcity messaging to motivate immediate action, aligning with the aggressive sales approach.
- Social Proof Integration: Showcase simulated testimonials and proofs of income (textual and potentially image placeholders) to build trust and leverage the 'prova social' effect on prospective buyers.
- Ad Platform Tracking Integration: Integrate necessary pixels and tracking codes for Facebook Ads, TikTok Ads, and Instagram Ads to measure campaign performance and optimize traffic generation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, strong green (#009B3A) for call-to-action buttons and key interactive elements, evoking growth, prosperity, and the national spirit of Brazil.
- Background color: A deep, dark blue (#001C50) to provide a high-contrast, modern, and serious backdrop for the aggressive sales copy, reflecting the night sky of the Brazilian flag.
- Accent color: A brilliant, rich gold/yellow (#FFD700) for headlines, highlights, and urgent messages, symbolizing wealth and opportunity, standing out powerfully against the dark blue and green.
- Headline and body font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif, will be used for both to convey a precise, contemporary, and aggressive marketing feel for maximum readability and impact.
- Utilize clear, bold, and impactful icons (e.g., '🔒 Acesso imediato', '💳 Pagamento seguro', '⚡ Liberação instantânea') near CTAs and key information sections to reinforce psychological triggers.
- Modern, mobile-first layout featuring a large, dominant Video Sales Letter (VSL) player, prominently placed and uniformly styled CTA buttons, a clear urgency counter, and well-defined sections optimized for quick scanning and high conversion rates. A fixed, sticky CTA button will be present for mobile views.
- Subtle yet effective animations for buttons on hover/focus and minimal, smooth transitions between page sections to add dynamism without distracting from the core sales message.
